How it Plays

It's a snowball fight.
That's the whole pitch.

If you can build a snowman, you already know the rules. Here's everything you actually need to remember.

Pack & throw

Measure a toss with a piece of string, lob your snowball, and hope it lands. Range is something you eyeball — not a calculator.

Nobody gets knocked out

Take enough hits and you're Frozen: warm up by the fire, sit out a turn, hop right back in. No characters ever leave the table.

The cold is the clock

Each round the chill creeps up a notch. When your whole crew is too cold to keep going, you call it and head inside for cocoa.

★

Last clique outside wins

No kill counts. No dice tables to memorize. Whichever team is still out in the snow gets bragging rights — until tomorrow, anyway.

No rulebook the size of a phone book. No one dies. Truly, it's just a snow day with sides.
